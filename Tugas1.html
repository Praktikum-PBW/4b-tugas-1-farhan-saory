Introduction

Strawberries are sweet supple red fruits with surfaces are seed-studded. They come from a low-growing plant belonging to genus Fragaria of the rose (Rosaceae) family. Enclosing white flowers, lobed foliage, and runners. They originate all through the north temperate zones.
